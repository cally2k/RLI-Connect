Retailer

The Retail & Leisure sectors have experienced huge challenges across the world as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic and will continue to face unprecedented market disruptions for the foreseeable future. In order to survive and thrive in a post Covid world, the retail industry needs to evolve and seek opportunities that have arisen. This year's event aims to connect you with the right partners to facilitate transformation and expansion strategies in a ‘New Era’.

Why Should I Take Part?

As we all come to terms with new shared realities, staying connected has never been more prevalent. Save time and money with all your pre-arranged meetings under one roof. As it’s a hybrid event, those who are longing to meet in person have the power of face-to-face whilst those still unable to travel can take advantage of the virtual element, enabling you to explore new markets, make new connections with potential franchise partners, owners, developers and investors, form strategic partnerships and sign those deals, all from one physical or digital location…one event two experiences.

How Can I Take Part?

This year’s event is complimentary for Retailers, Entertainment Concepts, Leisure and F&B operators. Attend the event by completing our Retailer/Entertainment/Leisure Operator Registration Form. Once registered you will be contacted by a member of the team to co-ordinate your schedule. You will conduct meetings with owners/developers in their private meeting rooms and with franchise partners and other affiliated companies in a central location. If you are utilising the virtual element we will ensure you are connected with your pre-arranged one to one meetings at the allotted times.

As a Retailer / Entertainment Concept / Leisure Operator / Franchise Operator looking to expand or transform your brand, you will already have strategies in place to either revolutionize your brand or expand into new markets, let us know who you would like to meet and we will connect you with the right companies to facilitate your ambitions whilst securing that all-important deal.

What Do I Need To Prepare?

Making sure that you plan your schedule in advance and research the developers / delegates that you wish to meet prior to the event is extremely important. If you do not pre-arrange meetings then you will not reap the benefits as developers and delegates tend to have no time for additional meetings.
